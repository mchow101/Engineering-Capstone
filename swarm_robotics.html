Swarm Robotics Mapping

Swarm robotics is the use of a large number of robots to complete a task that a single robot could not. In our design, the robots have three major functions: moving, communicating, and sensing. Using these three abilities in conjunction with each other, we aim to use the swarm to map out the layout of our high school's campus.
