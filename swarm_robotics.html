Swarm Robotics Mapping

Swarm robotics is the use of a large number of robots to complete a task which a single robot could not. In our design, the robots have three major functions: moving, communicating, and sensing. Using these three abilities in conjunction, we aim to use the swarm to map out the layout of our high school's campus.

Formal Research Question

How can we use a fundamental understanding of both robotic and swarm technologies to create a map of all accessible areas?
